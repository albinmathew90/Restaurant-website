A Decade of Pure Vegetarian Excellence

The Meridian embarked on its culinary journey in 2012, fueled by a passion to serve authentic, soul-satisfying Indian vegetarian cuisine. Thanks to the overwhelming love from our community, we have grown from a single kitchen into a celebrated destination for food lovers across the region.

At The Meridian, we prioritize the purity of our ingredients and the traditional retail experience at our store. We emphasize the use of hand-ground spices and time-honored cooking techniques, ensuring a world-class, authentic Indian experience for every guest, every time they visit us.

We believe that vegetarian food should be a celebration of flavors. Our chefs use traditional clay ovens (Tandoors) and secret family spice blends to ensure every bite is a journey through India's rich culinary history.
